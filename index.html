Abuja, Nigeria.

Abuja is the capital city of Nigeria, located right in the center of the country. It covers about 1,769 square kilometers and sits within the Federal Capital Territory (FCT). Designed to unite the nation, Abuja is known as the Centre of Unity. It is a city where people from different ethnic groups live, work, and grow together in peace. The city stands out for its good road network, solid infrastructure, and clean environment. It offers a calm and organized setting, free from the stress of overcrowded cities. Abuja is also a center of peace, beauty, and progress. From its peaceful weather to its well-planned layout, the city supports focus, learning, business, and national development.
