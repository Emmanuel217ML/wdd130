History

Our water rafting company was founded with a passion for adventure and a deep respect for the rich history of rafting, Inspired by pioneering expeditions such as those on the Salmon River in the 1940s and the transformative developments of the mid-20th century.

A sport that has evolved from indigenous survival techniques to a globally celebrated recreational activity. Our commitment to growth led us to expand our offerings with new routes and environmentally responsible practices, enhancing the sustainability of every trip
